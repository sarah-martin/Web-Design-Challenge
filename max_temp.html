Maximum Temperature

This graph shows a specific arch along the equator related to maximum temperature. The descending curve after a latitude of zero indicates that cities in the Northern Hempisphere do not have as hot of temperatures as the Southern Hempisphere. Additionally, this scatter plot indicates that along the equator and surrounding areas have in particular the hottest temperates throughout the year.
